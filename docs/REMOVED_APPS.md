# Removed Apps Audit

The consolidated suite intentionally retains only seven apps. The following source apps were removed from the active manifest and app tree before the old repositories are retired.

## Removed from `Brickfielder/micro-apps`

- `cashpoint-coach`
- `demo-timer`
- `email-composer`
- `mail-workflow-simulator`

The original `emotion-chat`, `freshmarket-budget`, and `neglect-reader` folders were not retained under their old slugs. Their kept functionality now lives under `emotion-chats`, `fresh-market`, and `left-anchor-reader`.
