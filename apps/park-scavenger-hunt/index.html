Park Scavenger Hunt

Goal: Find 10 scattered items in a virtual park environment.

This task is designed to train and assess **visual scanning** for patients with left visuo-spatial neglect.

  • Use W/S/A/D or arrow keys (or touch buttons) to walk and look around.
  • Turn your head and camera to the LEFT to find hidden objects.
  • Use the **⚓ Left Sky Arrow** to show a guide pointing left in the sky.
